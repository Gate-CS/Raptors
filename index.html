In this website I will have bird calls of different raptors sp that you can identify them in the wild. In case you don't know, raptors are hawks, falcons, eagles, vultures, and owls.

#1 Cooper's Hawk

This is a cooper's hawk. It has a wingspan of 2ft, is about 14in long, and weighs 11 pounds. It's diet consists of of rodents and small birds. A cooper's hawk's top speed is 54mph. Below is how a cooper's hawk sounds.

#2 Merlin

This is a Merlin (also known as a pigeon hawk), a small type of falcon. They have a wingspan of about 23 inches, and weigh about 200 grams. Their diet generally contains small birds, insects, and some small reptils. A merlin's top speed is 30 mph. Below is how a Merlin sounds.

#3 Golden Eagle

The eagle I will show in this website is a golden eagle. Golden eagles are golden brown, and weigh aproximately 11 pounds. Their wingspan is around 7ft, and they eat mostly small mammals such as rabbits, ground squirrels, and prairie dogs. They will also occaisonally eat large animals such as seals, coyotes, badgers, and other birds like owls and falcons.
